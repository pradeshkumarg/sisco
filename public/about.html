Welcome to SISCO, a family run business founded in 1999.



We have come a long way since our inception. When we first started out, our passion for frozen exotic seafood drove us to start our own business. Having played a lead role in developing this niche market in both UK and Europe, we are now well established in the ethnic seafood sector as importers and purveyors of frozen exotic seafood.

However, we are not just about seafood. We are a food and beverage company and an ever-growing one at that.

Our aim? Simple. To provide superior quality, great value products whilst delivering first class service which makes a genuine positive difference to people’s lives.

We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you.
